Welcome to ScentLock
Your Personal Bubble of Comfort.

ScentLock is a sleek, wearable necklace designed to create a personal
scent bubble around you shielding your senses from unpleasant odors in crowded, strong-smelling environments. Using a discreet scent diffuser powered by replaceable fragrance capsules, ScentLock releases a subtle, calming aroma of your choice. Whether you’re on public transport, in a hospital, or simply passing through a smelly area,
ScentLock helps you breathe easy and stay at ease wherever life takes you.
